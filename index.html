Industry Timeline
2025
US newspaper count drops below 1,250 for first time. Digital subscriptions cross 14M industry-wide.
2024
NYT hits 10M digital subs milestone. AI-generated summaries begin threatening publisher traffic.
2023
Generative AI erupts — publishers scramble to license content or litigate.
2022
Alden Global becomes largest US newspaper owner by title count.
2021
Tribune Publishing acquired by Alden for $633M. Digital ad revenue surpasses print for first time.

COVID accelerates print decline. 70+ papers close. Subscriptions spike on pandemic news.
2019
GateHouse-Gannett $1.4B merger creates largest US newspaper chain (260+ dailies).
2018
"Trump Bump" subscriptions peak. WaPo hits 1.5M digital subs.
2005
